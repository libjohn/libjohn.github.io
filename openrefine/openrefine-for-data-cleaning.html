Note
1. The hands-on approach includes a reliance on the data-science notion of “hacking” (not “cracking”). Pay attention to what you are seeing, what you are typing, what happens, what transforms, the end-result vis-a-vis the initial state of the data.
2. Learn by doing
3. Learn best by working on your own project
